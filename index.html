School Canteen

Was Established on 13 Dec 2008

For the convenience of staff, students and parents visiting, we have built this small cafeteria. Exclusively bought items are: Samosa (2 for 15 Rupees), Panneer Pizza (4 slices for 35 Rupess) and Cold Choco Moussee (1 cup for 25 Rupees)

School Sports

Sports were added to the campus at 24 Jan 1999.

Along with studies, play is also equally important for children. So we have added P.E Classes to Monday, Wednesday,Thursday and Friday for more play time. Sports List are: Cricket (Taught by Mr.Inesh), Football (Taught by Mr.Atharva), Badminton (Taught by Ms.Harini) and Yoga (Taught by Mr.Rahul).
